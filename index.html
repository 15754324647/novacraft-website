Hangzhou Gap Light Technology Co., Ltd. is a technology company specializing in software development and AI-powered solutions.

公司以"平台 + 多产品"架构运营，通过前沿的软件工程与 AI 能力，构建安全、实用、可扩展的数字产品。服务涵盖技术研发、技术咨询与数字化解决方案。

We build safe, practical, and scalable digital products with a "platform + multi-product" architecture, powered by cutting-edge software engineering and AI capabilities.
